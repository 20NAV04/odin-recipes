Easy Pinoy Chicken Curry Recipe

Description

Chicken curry is a popular dish and comfort food found in the Philippines. As opposed to its Indian ancestors, Philippine curry relies less on spices and derives its flavors from the time spent in simmering vegetables. Not only does it make the dish taste milder, it also makes it substantially easier to preapare as the ingredients needed for it are easy to procure.

Speaking of ingredients, the recipes will make use of the following:

  • 1 Whole Chicken
  • 2 Cups of Coconut Milk
  • Curry Mix or Curry Powder
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 3 Tablespoons of Fish Sauce
  • 5 Large Potatoes (Quartered)
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic and 1 Onion (Diced)
  • 2 Thumbs of Ginger (Julienned)
  • 3 Green Bell Peppers

Steps

  1. Prepare the necessary ingredients
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry potatoes until outsides are crusty and set aside on a different plate
  3. Fry chicken until the skin is browned and afterwards add the cut onion and garlic
    • This is done so that the chicken won't breakdown during the simmering process
  4. Once onion starts browning, add ginger and fish sauce
  5. Add water enough to completely submerge all portions of meat
  6. Once boiling, add the potatoes, bell peper, and the curry mix and bring to a simmer
  7. Add the coconut milk and stir continuously for 3 minutes
    • Stirring is done to prevent the milk from coagulating
  8. Season accordingly with salt and pepper
  9. Serve on a bowl and enjoy
