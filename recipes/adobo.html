Easy Chicken Adobo Recipe

Description

Chicken Adobo is one of the most recognizable Filipino dishes that is loved by all. Its recipe calls for the use of one of the staple ingredients of Filipino cuisine--soy sauce. The dish itself has lots of iterations that vary per household making it a truly one-of-a-kind dish.

The following are the ingredients required to follow along with this recipe:

  • 1 Whole Chicken
  • 1/2 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 3 Laurel Leaves
  • 5 Eggs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 Tablespoons of Sugar (Any)
  • 4 Tablespoons of Vinegar (Any)
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic and 1 Whole Onion (Diced)
  • 4 Large Potatoes (Quartered)

Steps

  1. Heat oil on a pan
  2. Lightly fry potatoes until outsides are crusty then set aisde
  3. Fry chicken until skin is browned
  4. Add onion and garlic, sautee until garlic starts to brown
  5. Add vinegar and add enough water to submerge chicken
  6. Add soy sauce and eggs (still in shell)
  7. Once boiling, add potatoes, laurel leaves, and sugar
  8. Switch to low-heat, season with salt and pepper
  9. Serve on a bowl and enjoy
